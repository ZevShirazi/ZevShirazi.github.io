## About me

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Oxford under Sam Staton. I work on the theory of monads, and I’m interested in their role in:

- Semantics for computational effects in programming languages  
- Universal algebra and its generalisations  
- Categorical logic and program logics  

Recently I had a six month research visit working with Ichiro Hasuo at the National Institute of Informatics.

Outside of academia I enjoy kayaking, world cinema, playing jazz trombone, and contemporary piano music.
